About Me

My full name is Ammad Mohammad Shaikh. I was born and raised in Chicago. I originally after graduating highschool wanted to pursue a career as an architect however I graduated in 2008 right when the housing market crash leaving architects with one of the highest umemployment rates in the country ultimately forcing me to ditch my dream. Since then I have tried pursuing careers in Healthcare, and Sales with both leaving me unsatisfied, until I came to coding which in a sense allows me to use my hands to build and create something; similar to architecture.


I cannot wait to truly dive head first into a fulfilling career as a developer whether it be building websites or mobile apps. Hopefully if I'm lucky I will one day build the next Amzon, Ebay, Youtube.
